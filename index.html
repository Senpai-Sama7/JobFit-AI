How It Works

How JobFit AI Empowers Your Job Search

  • 1

    Upload Your Resume

    Upload your resume (PDF, DOCX, TXT, etc). We’ll instantly parse your experience, education, and skills.

  • 2

    AI Profile Analysis

    Advanced AI analyzes your resume, categorizing your technical, soft, and domain-specific skills and computing an ATS compatibility score.

  • 3

    Personalized Role Recommendations

    Our AI suggests relevant job roles that fit your profile using real-time job market data, showing “fit” scores for each match.

  • 4

    Tailored Resume Generation

    Select a job posting, and JobFit AI generates a tailored resume optimized for that role – with improvement suggestions.
