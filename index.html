You have questions, right?

We have answers to the questions you most often ask us.

How does JobFit AI improve my chances of getting hired?

JobFit AI gives you a crucial advantage by ensuring your resume is optimized for the automated systems (ATS) that most companies use. It analyzes your resume against specific job descriptions, suggests keywords, and helps you highlight your most relevant skills. This significantly increases the likelihood that your resume will be seen by a human recruiter, leading to more interviews.

Is my resume data and personal information secure?

Absolutely. We take your privacy and data security very seriously. We use industry-standard encryption and security protocols (like PostgreSQL with Drizzle ORM) to protect all user data. Your resume and personal information are for your use only and will never be shared without your explicit consent.

What file formats can I upload?

JobFit AI supports a wide range of common document formats to make it easy for you. You can upload your resume as a PDF, DOCX, TXT, MD, RTF, and ODT file. The maximum file size is 10MB.

Can I cancel my subscription at any time?

Yes, you have complete flexibility. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your monthly or yearly plan at any time directly from your account dashboard. There are no long-term contracts or hidden cancellation fees.
