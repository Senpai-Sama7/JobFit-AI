How It Works

1. Upload Your Resume

Begin by uploading your resume (PDF, DOCX, TXT, etc.). Our system parses the content to extract your contact info, experience, education, and skills. Uploading is easy with a drag-and-drop interface.

2. AI Analysis

Behind the scenes, JobFit AI analyzes your resume. It profiles your technical, soft, and domain-specific skills and evaluates ATS compatibility. You get an ATS score indicating how well your resume fares with Applicant Tracking Systems.

3. Role Recommendations

Using semantic matching, our AI suggests relevant job roles that fit your profile. You’ll see a list of recommended positions with “fit” scores, so you know which opportunities are best for you.

4. Tailor & Improve

Pick a target job and let JobFit AI tailor your resume to that posting. The platform generates a customized version of your resume optimized for the specific job description. It also provides actionable improvement suggestions to boost your resume’s effectiveness.
